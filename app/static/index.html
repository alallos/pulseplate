How PulsePlate works

How it works

  • Connect Oura — We use your ring’s sleep, recovery, and activity data (with your permission).
  • Set preferences — Diet style, calorie target, goals, and allergies shape your plans.
  • Get your plan — AI generates a daily or weekly meal plan and grocery list tailored to your recovery.

What we store

We store your Oura tokens (to fetch your data), your saved preferences, and your generated meal plans so you can revisit them. We do not sell your data.

Your control

You can Disconnect Oura or Delete my data anytime from the connection area below (once connected). Disconnecting removes our access to Oura; deleting removes all stored plans and preferences.

For details, see privacy & deletion policy.
