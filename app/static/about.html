How PulsePlate works

PulsePlate turns your wearable signals into meal plans you can actually follow. No spreadsheets. No guesswork. Just a plan shaped by your recovery and goals.

The flow

  • Connect Oura (OAuth) to read sleep, recovery, HRV, resting heart rate, and activity.
  • Set preferences for diet style, calorie target, goals, allergies, and units.
  • Generate your plan with AI for daily meals or a 5–7 day weekly batch.

Privacy & control

We store the minimum needed to deliver your plans (tokens, preferences, and plan history). You can disconnect Oura or delete all your data anytime.
