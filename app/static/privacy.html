PulsePlate

Privacy & deletion

This is a short, plain-language summary. It is not legal advice.

What we store

  • Oura access tokens (so we can fetch your biometrics).
  • Your preferences (diet style, calorie target, goals, allergies, units).
  • Your generated meal plans (so you can reopen them later).

What we don’t do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We don’t show your personal data publicly.

Your control

You can disconnect Oura or delete your data from the app (buttons in the connection area). Deleting removes your saved plans and your user record.

Security

Access is protected with a JWT session cookie after Oura connect. OAuth state is signed using your server secret.
